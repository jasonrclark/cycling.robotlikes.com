Robot Likes Beans 🚲

What are beans?

Beans are seeds from the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legumes. They are one of the longest-cultivated plants, providing a rich source of protein, fiber, and complex carbohydrates.

Why do cyclists love beans?

Beans are an excellent fuel for endurance athletes. They provide slow-releasing carbohydrates that sustain energy over long rides, along with plant-based protein to support muscle recovery.

Popular bean varieties

  • Black beans — High in protein and antioxidants.
  • Kidney beans — Rich in iron and folate.
  • Chickpeas — Versatile and packed with fiber.
  • Lentils — Fast-cooking and full of complex carbs.
  • Coffee beans — The cyclist's true best friend. ☕

Fun bean fact

A single cup of cooked beans provides roughly 15 grams of protein and 15 grams of fiber — plenty of energy for your next ride!
